About Us

We're You Swine, a DIY punk rock band hailing from Gainesville, Florida. With deep roots in the local scene and a shared love of loud music and raucous drums, we write all our songs together as a group. Our first EP captures the intensity and honesty that drives us. Raw riffs, fast drums and lyrics that confront the world head on. We stand firmly against hate and intolerance and believe punk rock should always be a space for everyone.
